OUR STORY

Situated in Ksamil, within 400 m of Ksamil Beach and 700 m of Coco Beach, Vila Ani features accommodation with a garden and free WiFi as well as free private parking for guests who drive. Boasting family rooms, this property also provides guests with a terrace. Certain units at the property have a patio with a garden view.

Rooms are complete with a private bathroom, while some rooms at the guest house also boast a seating area.

Popular points of interest near Vila Ani include Ksamil Beach 7, Bora Bora Beach and Ksamil Beach 9. The nearest airport is Ioannina Airport, 109 km from the accommodation.
